  • Research interests

My research interests are Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning, Data Mining and Visualization with special emphasis on peer review data. Working in a research team on peer-review system, my current research is mainly focusing on building automated components for evaluating quantitative feedback (peer ratings) and qualitative comments from peers to student papers and/or student argument diagrams. In particular, I have been working on automatically identifying localization and solution in peer reviews, and predicting peer rating validity.

In my undergraduate school, I gained research experience on parallel algorithms, and genetic algorithms.
